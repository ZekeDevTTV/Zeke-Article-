Official Edicts

Developed by the esteemed id Software and championed by Bethesda Softworks, Doom: The Dark Ages marks a confirmed new chapter in the saga.

Prepare to journey back in time, for this prequel to Doom (2016) and Doom Eternal unveils the origin story of the legendary DOOM Slayer.

The game's grand unveiling at the Xbox Games Showcase in June 2024 sent shockwaves through the gaming community, a testament to the franchise's enduring legacy.

Rumored Rampage

While official pronouncements remain silent, whispers on the wind and tantalizing leaks suggest a potential shift. The frenetic gunplay that defined recent DOOM titles might give way to a more measured approach in Doom: The Dark Ages. Imagine a dance of brutal melee combat, punctuated by the thunderous roar of a well-placed BFG.

The Slayer's arsenal could transform to reflect the era. Think gleaming swords, axes that cleave demons in two, and war hammers that pulverize bone. However, whispers also hint at the return of iconic firearms like the Super Shotgun – a relic from a future yet to unfold.

The rumored setting plunges you into a grim medieval world, twisted and corrupted by demonic influence. Brace yourself to face a chilling menagerie – horrifying new demonic monstrosities alongside familiar foes from the classic DOOM bestiary. All rendered in the latest idTech engine, promising a horrifyingly beautiful spectacle.
